APEX DIGITAL SOLUTIONS — EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK (EXCERPTS) & INTERNAL FAQ
Fictional sample document for demos. Last updated: Q1 2026.

================================================================================
SECTION A — REMOTE & HYBRID WORK
================================================================================
Eligible roles may work remotely up to three (3) days per week with manager approval.
Core collaboration hours are 10:00–15:00 in the employee’s local time zone unless a client contract specifies otherwise.
Employees must use company-approved VPN and endpoint protection on any device used for Apex work.

================================================================================
SECTION B — PAID TIME OFF (PTO)
================================================================================
Full-time employees accrue PTO at a rate of 1.25 days per month for the first two years of service, then 1.67 days per month thereafter, capped at 25 accrued days.
PTO requests require at least five (5) business days’ notice unless approved as an exception by HR.
Up to five (5) unused PTO days may carry over into the next calendar year; any excess is forfeited unless the carryover policy exception is granted in writing by HR.

================================================================================
SECTION C — CODE OF CONDUCT (SUMMARY)
================================================================================
Apex is committed to a respectful, inclusive workplace. Discrimination, harassment, or retaliation based on protected characteristics is prohibited and may result in disciplinary action up to termination.
Confidential client and company information must not be disclosed outside approved channels. Social posts about clients require Legal review before publication.

================================================================================
SECTION D — INTERNAL FAQ (HR)
================================================================================
Q: Who do I contact for payroll corrections?
A: Email payroll@apex-digital.example with your employee ID and pay period. Responses are within two business days.

Q: How do I submit an expense report?
A: Use the Finance Portal (Expenses tab). Attach receipts over $25. Reimbursement is processed on the 15th and last day of each month.

Q: What is the dress code for client sites?
A: Business casual unless the client specifies otherwise. When in doubt, ask your project lead at least 24 hours before the visit.

Q: Are side projects allowed?
A: Yes, if they do not compete with Apex services, do not use company resources, and are disclosed to your manager via the Outside Activities form.

Q: What is the policy on AI tools for customer deliverables?
A: Customer-facing work may use approved AI assistants listed on the Engineering wiki. Do not paste confidential data into unapproved tools. When unsure, open a ticket with Information Security.
